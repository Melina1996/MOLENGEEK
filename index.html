WHAT IS MOLENGEEK?

MolenGeek is located in the heart of Molenbeek, Brussels. Our mission is to make the technology sector accessible to anyone wishing to ride the digital wave, regardless of background or level of training.

We are made up of three main pillars: a coworking space, long- and short-term training programs and dynamic events, such as Hackathons, Geektalks and more.
