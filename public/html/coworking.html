COWORKING SPACE

"Le cowork", as it's known, is the head office of the MolenGeek community, and welcomes anyone interested in learning web development, as well as entrepreneurs who want to launch their own startup in the digital sector, and trainees from the Coding School.

Every day, coworkers meet new people, network, forge professional links, learn new entrepreneurial techniques and help each other out.

A shared workspace open to all .

The coworking space is equipped with everything you need to work in the best possible conditions (tea, coffee, fully-equipped kitchen, lockers, printers, high-speed wifi connection, etc.).

Members can contribute by getting involved in the community in a variety of ways:

  • taking part in events
  • brainstorm with other members
  • sharing knowledge and skills
  • collaborate with other coworkers
  • helping each other
  • and lots more...

OPENING HOURS

The coworking space is open Monday to Friday from 8am to 6pm, Saturdays and Sundays from 10am to 6pm, and outside these hours on request only.
