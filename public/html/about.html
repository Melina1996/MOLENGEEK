WHAT IS MOLENGEEK?

MolenGeek is a technology ecosystem that makes technology accessible to everyone, regardless of their academic background or experience. This initiative rests on three key pillars: the incubator, courses and inspiring events.

3 PILLARS

MolenGeek's training programs cover a wide range of technological fields, from computer programming and cybersecurity to digital marketing and artificial intelligence. These programs are designed to enhance the skills of job seekers and prepare them for promising careers in the technology sector.

MolenGeek's incubator provides tangible support to budding entrepreneurs by providing resources, experienced mentors and an environment conducive to the growth of their startups.

Events organized by MolenGeek, such as hackathons, foster creativity, innovation and collaborative learning within the technology community.
